About Sleep Stores

Creating Quality Furniture for 20 Years

Sleep Stores has been a leading furniture manufacturer for the past 20 years, specializing in creating innovative and versatile furniture. Our unique selling points include our own manufacturing unit and our ability to offer furniture options such as cots that can also act as chairs and sofas. We have a large showroom with a wide range of products, including 50 types of pillows, and mattresses made from cotton, coir, foam, latex, and more. We also offer our own line of bedspreads. At Sleep Stores, we focus on building quality materials and creating furniture that lasts.
